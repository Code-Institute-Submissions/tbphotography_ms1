What I do!


I love taking photographs, and most of all I love taking photographs of animals! Whether it's taking photographs of my own cat (which may have an appearance in my gallery) or wild animals, I enjoy the unpredicability of their behaviour, and the reward of getting a great photograph!

I primarily take photos for commercial use, so if you need photos for a project, feel free to get in touch with me and see what I can do to help you!
