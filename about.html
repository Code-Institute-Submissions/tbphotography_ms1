About Me

Hi, my name is Taylor! I've been interested in photography since I can remember. My passion started when I was young; I used to pick up old cameras at fares and keep them on my shelves. My parents bought me my first working camera many years ago and I've been taking photos ever since!
I enjoy taking photographs of any kind, but my favourite subjects are nature, landscapes and animals. Check out my gallery to see some of my personal favourites.
I take pictures for clients who are looking for locational photoshoots for personal or professional use. See my "Contact Me" page for more info!
When I'm not taking photos I enjoy watching movies and exploring the UK, taking long countryside walks.
